# Aura_Orchestra

Aura_Orchestra is a governed, auditable, multi-model AI orchestration platform.

## Core Principles
- Human authority first (Director)
- Deterministic orchestration (Manager)
- Independent workers (Employees / Models)
- Formal scoring & penalties (Accountant)
- Continuous oversight (Auditor)
- Enforced rules & lifecycle control (HR)

## Architecture
- Docker-first
- MCP-compatible
- Postgres as system-of-record
- Multi-OS (Windows / Ubuntu)
- Self-healing containers
